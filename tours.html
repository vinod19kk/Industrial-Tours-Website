Special Tours

Nagarjun Sagar Dam

It is built across the Krishna River at Nagarjuna Sagar which is in Nalgonda District. Best time to visit is around September-October.From INR 999

Varahi Power Plant

It is built across the Varahi river. Power generation takes place underground. Varahi is Karnataka's first underground powerhouse.From INR 1599

Neyveli Power Plant

It is situated near lignite mines of Neyveli. It has two stations Stage-I and Stage-II.From INR 1999

Pavagada Solar Park

The park, known as Shakti Sthala is located in the Pavagada region of the Tumkur district. It is spread over 13,000 acres of land. The total capacity of this solar park is said to be 2,000 megawatts.From INR 1799
